Jamiahus Walton

Assistant Professor
Department Industrial Engineering
Clemson University
Dr. Walton obtained his Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction from Iowa State University in 2019. He received his master’s degree in industrial engineering from Iowa State University and his bachelor’s degree in physics from Millikin University. He is interested in developing Intelligent Team Tutoring Systems (ITTSs) that effectively and efficiently tutors teams in various domains. His research interests include computer-supported cooperative work, team training, and virtual collaboration.

Maya Rucks

Research Assistant
Department Industrial Engineering

Maya Rucks is a Ph.D student in Industrial Engineering at Clemson University. She received her certificate for Engineering and Science Education at Clemson University, master’s degree in Industrial Engineering at Louisiana Tech University and her bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from the University of Louisiana Monroe. Her research interests include tools and technology for STEM education, human factors in design, and decision making.
